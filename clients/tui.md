# 🎧 Best Tui Clients for Selfhost Music

A curated list of recommended Tui clients compatible with **Selfhost Music** and other Subsonic-compatible servers.

---

## ⭐ Recommended Clients

### jellyfin-tui

- **API Support:** ✅ Jellyfin
- **License:** GPL-3.0
- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes
- **Price:** 🆓 Free
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Status:** 🟢 Actively maintained
- **Highlights:**
  - stream your music from Jellyfin
  - lyrics with autoscroll (Jellyfin > 10.9)
  - Msixel cover image, courtesy of ratatui-image
  - Smart playlist editor (Navidrome)
  - Synchronized and unsynchronized lyrics support
  - vim-style keybindings

---

### SubTUI

- **API Support:** ✅ Subsonic
- **License:** MIT license
- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes
- **Price:** 🆓 Free
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Status:** 🟢 Actively maintained
- **Highlights:**
  - mpv to support various audio codecs and reliable playback
  - keybinds, color themes, and settings via a simple TOML file
  - ReplayGain Support: Built-in support for Track and Album volume normalization
  - Gapless Playback: Enjoy your favorite albums exactly as intended with smooth, uninterrupted transitions
  - MPRIS Support: Control SubTUI from any media widget on Linux/FreeBSD
  - Discord Integration: Show off what you're listening to with built-in Discord Rich Presence
