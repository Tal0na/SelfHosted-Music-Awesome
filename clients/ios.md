# 🍎 Best iOS Clients for Self-Hosted Music Servers

Clients compatible with self-hosted music servers such as:

- Navidrome
- Jellyfin
- Subsonic
- Airsonic
- OpenSubsonic compatible servers

---

## ⭐ Recommended Apps

### play:Sub

- **Support:** ✅ Subsonic / ✅ OpenSubsonic / ✅ Navidrome
- **License:** Proprietary
- **Open Source:** ❌ No
- **Price:** 💰 Paid (one-time purchase)
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Highlights:**
  - One of the most popular Subsonic clients for iOS
  - Offline downloads
  - Smart playlists
  - Background playback
  - Very polished UI

---

### Amperfy

- **Support:** ✅ Subsonic / ✅ OpenSubsonic / ✅ Navidrome
- **License:** GPL-3.0
- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes
- **Price:** 🆓 Free
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
- **Highlights:**
  - Native iOS design
  - Offline playback
  - Background streaming
  - Fully open source

---

### Substreamer

- **Support:** ✅ Subsonic / ✅ Navidrome
- **License:** Proprietary
- **Open Source:** ❌ No
- **Price:** 🆓 Free / optional premium
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
- **Highlights:**
  - Simple interface
  - Streaming + offline playback
  - Easy server configuration

---

### iSub

- **Support:** ✅ Subsonic / ✅ Navidrome
- **License:** Proprietary
- **Open Source:** ❌ No
- **Price:** 💰 Paid
- **Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆
