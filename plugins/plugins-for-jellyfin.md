# 🔌 Best Jellyfin Music Plugins

A small collection of useful plugins and integrations that enhance the **Jellyfin music experience** with features like lyrics, scrobbling, and external service integrations.

---

## 🧩 Jellyfin Lyrics Plugin  

🔗 <https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-plugin-lyrics>  

- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes  
- **Price:** Free  

Automatically fetches and displays **song lyrics** from online providers while listening to music.

---

## 🎧 ListenBrainz Plugin  

🔗 <https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-plugin-listenbrainz>  

- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes  
- **Price:** Free  

Scrobbles your listening activity from Jellyfin to **ListenBrainz**, helping track your music history.

---

## 📊 Last.fm Plugin  

🔗 <https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-plugin-lastfm>  

- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes  
- **Price:** Free  

Integrates **Last.fm** with Jellyfin for scrobbling, recommendations, and music statistics.

---

## 🎼 MusicBrainz Metadata Plugin  

🔗 <https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-plugin-musicbrainz>  

- **Open Source:** ✅ Yes  
- **Price:** Free  

Improves music metadata using **MusicBrainz**, helping organize artists, albums, and tracks.

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- Plugins can be installed directly from the **Jellyfin plugin catalog**.
- Availability may depend on your Jellyfin server version.
- Always check the repository for installation instructions and updates.
